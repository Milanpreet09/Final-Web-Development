About Me

Harminder Singh, popularly known as Harminder Dulowal is a Professional Vegetarian bodybuilder based in Punjab. Sounds interesting right? Yes, you got it right. Smashing every misconception about plant-based protein Harminder Singh emerged as an epitome of fitness by becoming an International Vegetarian bodybuilder, not just this he went on adding more feathers on his cap with the title of Mr. Asia Gold Medalist, FIF India President, FIF International Judge, Founder of Red Power energy drink, All India Intervarsity Silver Medalist, Mr. World South Africa 2015, WFF International certified Judge, Mr. Asia 4th in Singapore 2016, Muscle war 2017 in Singapore and Founder of Red Gym in Kapurthala Punjab. His invincible success is indeed awe inspiring. 35 years old Harminder was born and raised in a Sikh family from Punjab began his journey as a bodybuilder from participating in schools and colleges where he started receiving the title as a winner. He had completed his graduation from Khalsa college Jalandhar with B.P. Ed and M.P. Ed from Punjab university, Patiala. During this glorious time he had visited Singapore, Dubai, South Africa, Australia, Canada and other countries. He completed his final training as a bodybuilder in Singapore and soon after achieving this milestone he was rewarded with the honour of being a judge of the FIF (Fitness International Foundation).He credits vegetarianism for his muscular physique that makes him an outstanding example of building body through vegetarianism. One thing that’s apparent is whether it is a bodybuilding or any other profession the ethical principle of commitment, discipline and a dedicated diet regime are all that is needed for you to remain at the top of your game. Now he successfully runs his own Gym in the name of Red Gym in Kapurthala Punjab where he provides Gym trainer certification classes. In addition to this he also operates his own company in the name of Red Power, an Energy drink.
